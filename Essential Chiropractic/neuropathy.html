Understanding Neuropathy

What Is Peripheral Neuropathy?

Peripheral neuropathy is nerve damage affecting the nerves outside the brain and spinal cord. It causes debilitating pain, numbness, and loss of function — and typically worsens over time if left untreated.

Most patients have tried medications or injections with little lasting relief. Our approach is different — we identify the root cause and address it directly, without drugs or surgery.
