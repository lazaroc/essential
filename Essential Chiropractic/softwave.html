What Is SoftWave Therapy?

SoftWave TRT uses electro-hydraulically produced acoustic waves to trigger a powerful healing response in the body. It mimics cellular injury at the treatment site — signaling your body's own stem cells to migrate to the area and accelerate natural regeneration.

The treatment is completely atraumatic to tissues and cells. It's comfortable, fast, and requires no recovery time — most sessions take 15 minutes or less.
