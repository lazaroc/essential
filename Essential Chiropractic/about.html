Meet the Doctor

Dr. Kristen
Ras-Davis, D.C.

Doctor of Chiropractic · Co-Founder, Essential Chiropractic

B.S. Kinesiology — Univ. of Illinois D.C. Summa Cum Laude — Logan University Certified Advanced TRT

Dr. Kristen grew up in La Grange, Illinois — a suburb of Chicago. She attended the University of Illinois at Champaign-Urbana, where she earned her Bachelor's degree in Kinesiology. During her junior year, a class assignment led her to shadow a local chiropractor. She was immediately struck by the atmosphere, the positivity, and the remarkable patient stories she witnessed — and knew she had found her calling.

She went on to Logan University in Chesterfield, MO, where she earned her second Bachelor's degree and her Doctorate of Chiropractic — graduating Summa Cum Laude. Logan is also where she met Dr. Greg.

During chiropractic school, Dr. Kristen was mentored by exceptional doctors and earned an internship at Stewart Chiropractic Center in Hendersonville, TN — just outside Nashville. It was there she truly fell in love with chiropractic. She stayed for three more years after graduation, deepening her skills and experience before reuniting with Dr. Greg to open Essential Chiropractic.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Greg
Davis, D.C.

Doctor of Chiropractic · Co-Founder, Essential Chiropractic

D.C. — Logan University Board Certified Certified Advanced TRT Former Collegiate Athlete

Dr. Greg is a central Illinois native with a lifelong passion for health and performance. As a youth and collegiate athlete, he experienced chiropractic firsthand — it kept him healthy and competing at his best. That personal experience ignited a drive to share those same benefits with others.

He earned his Doctorate of Chiropractic from Logan University, College of Chiropractic, where he dedicated himself to weight training and developing clinical expertise alongside Dr. Kristen.

After graduating, Dr. Greg joined Innate Chiropractic in Newburgh, New York — one of the largest chiropractic practices in the Northeast region. During his three-year tenure there, his appreciation for chiropractic deepened as he witnessed daily how powerfully the body heals when nerve interference is removed. He returned to the Midwest ready to build something meaningful alongside Dr. Kristen in Lee's Summit.

Our Story

Why We Chose Lee's Summit

After years of building their skills on opposite coasts — Dr. Kristen in Tennessee, Dr. Greg in New York — the two doctors got engaged and set out across the Midwest searching for a place to call home and start their practice together.

After exploring multiple cities, the answer was clear. Kansas City, and more specifically Lee's Summit, just felt like home. The community, the energy, the opportunity to truly make a difference — it all aligned with the life and practice they had envisioned.

Together, with combined experience, knowledge, and a shared passion for natural health, their goal is simple: to help every person and family in Lee's Summit achieve better health and a better quality of life through neurologically-based chiropractic care.

What We Believe

The Philosophy Behind Our Practice

Natural Health First

We believe the body has an innate ability to heal when the nervous system is free of interference. Our mission is to remove those barriers — without drugs or surgery — and let your body do what it was designed to do.

Care for the Whole Family

From newborns to seniors, we're proud to serve entire families in Lee's Summit. Every member of your household — at any stage of life — deserves access to safe, gentle, and effective chiropractic care.

Community-Rooted Care

Lee's Summit is home — not just a market. Our mission is to build a healthier community for generations to come. We're invested in this city and the people who make it great, and we show up every day to prove it.
