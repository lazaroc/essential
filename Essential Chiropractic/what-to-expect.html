Your First Visit

A Clear, Comfortable Process

It is our goal to make you as comfortable as possible during every visit. From the moment you arrive, we walk you through each step — taking the time to understand your health needs so we can serve you as best we can.

Step1

New Patient Paperwork

All new practice members at Essential Chiropractic are asked to fill out the new practice member paperwork as thoroughly as possible. This helps us understand your health needs better, and allows us to serve you as best we can.

Step2

Office Tour

After completing your new member application, we take you on a brief educational tour of our office. Knowing your way around the practice is the first step in making every visit feel comfortable and familiar.

Step3

One-on-One Consultation

We sit down with you one-on-one to better understand your health needs. Here we discuss your concerns and goals for care, and learn about any traumas that might be contributing to your health concerns.

Step4

Scientific Chiropractic Assessment

Using our state-of-the-art assessment technology, we perform a thorough evaluation of your spinal health. This allows us to pinpoint if — and where — there are any areas of stress in your spine.

Step5

Digital X-Ray

Essential Chiropractic has a digital X-ray unit on site. If necessary, it allows us to see the exact health of your spine — giving us the opportunity to tailor our care to your specific health needs.

Step6

Orientation & Report of Findings

Your next visit begins with a brief report discussing our approach to your health needs and goals. We then review all the findings from your initial visit and discuss your individualized treatment plan.

Bring a friend or loved one to this visit
Step7

Your First Adjustment

At the conclusion of your report of findings, you have the opportunity to receive your first specific chiropractic adjustment — your first step toward optimal health. We look forward to partnering with you on your healing journey.
